Files Changed

Required State Categories Implemented

1. valid_no_signal
Definition:
Connector available, data fetched, scan executed, no signal found.

2. connector_unavailable
Definition:
Connector could not initialize or required exchange connector was missing, scan not executed.

3. data_fetch_failed
Definition:
Connector initialized, but required market data / regime data was not fetched, so signal scan was not executed.

4. signal_found
Definition:
Connector available, data fetched, scan executed, one or more signals were processed.
