E2 Traditional Futures Data Source Audit

Task type: Phase 2 infrastructure / data-source expansion only.
No strategy Track, outcome test, alpha research, crypto return merge, backtest, signal generation, V12 modification, or paid data purchase was performed.

Summary recommendation:
Yahoo Finance futures symbols are usable only conditionally as free daily broad cross-asset proxy data.
FUTURES LOGGER CONDITIONAL — USER ToS CONFIRMATION REQUIRED.

Yahoo Finance
- Candidate symbols reviewed:
  ES=F, NQ=F, RTY=F, CL=F, GC=F, HG=F, ZN=F, ZB=F, BTC=F, ETH=F.
- Availability:
  Public Yahoo Finance pages show futures quote coverage for ES=F, NQ=F, RTY=F and other futures. Search/indexed pages confirm BTC=F exists as CME Bitcoin futures. ETH=F should still be verified by live fetch before activation; do not assume it works.
- Data frequency:
  Daily OHLCV is feasible through Yahoo-style chart history endpoints if user confirms usage is acceptable.
- Historical coverage:
  Public pages show historical chart ranges, but exact complete depth by symbol must be confirmed by logger fetch. Continuous futures depth may vary by symbol.
- Free or paid:
  No payment is required for basic public Yahoo Finance pages/endpoints, but usage terms are not explicit enough here to activate unattended production logging without user confirmation.
- API/library requirement:
  No third-party library is required by the prototype. It uses the Yahoo chart endpoint through Python urllib.
- ToS caveat:
  Yahoo Finance usage is governed by Yahoo terms. Automated unattended downloading is not clearly authorized by this audit. The logger therefore requires an explicit --confirm-yahoo-tos flag and is not added to the active hourly workflow.
- Rollover limitation:
  ES=F, NQ=F, GC=F, CL=F, BTC=F and similar symbols are continuous/front-month proxy symbols. They can roll at contract expiry and may not be back-adjusted. Daily closes can contain rollover artifacts. They are acceptable only as broad cross-asset risk proxy data, not precise price-level research across rolls.
- Recommendation:
  usable only conditionally.

CME delayed quotes
- Source nature:
  CME delayed quote pages are official reference-style quote pages.
- Delay:
  CME states delayed quotes are delayed by at least 10 minutes.
- Limitation:
  Public delayed quote pages are not a clean automated historical daily OHLCV API for QuantCore. CME also warns website market data is reference-only and not a substitute for proper market data feeds.
- Recommendation:
  documentation/reference source only for now.

Databento
- Source nature:
  Professional market data vendor with futures coverage and historical products.
- Free or paid:
  Paid or credit-based access; no purchase was made.
- Strength:
  More appropriate than Yahoo for precise futures research, historical contract-level data, and reproducible roll logic.
- Limitation:
  Not used in E2 because this task forbids paid data purchase.
- Recommendation:
  optional paid future source.

Other free sources
- No other free, stable, no-account source was confirmed that is clearly superior to Yahoo for daily futures proxy logging.

Phase B conclusion
Traditional futures data can be integrated under E2 as a conditional proxy-data module, but production activation should wait for user confirmation of Yahoo Finance usage terms.
