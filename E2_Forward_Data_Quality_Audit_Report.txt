Missing-Hour Handling Policy

Existing missing hours remain missing.

Do not:
- backfill
- synthesize rows
- overwrite historical rows
- repair Pixel 4 data gaps
- infer missing values from later data

Missing hours should be surfaced through audit and health reports only.

Incomplete-Hour Handling Policy

Incomplete hours are retained as originally collected. They should be marked by audit logic and reviewed operationally, but not repaired by synthetic rows.

No-Backfill Rule

The forward dataset is an append-only evidence record. Historical gaps are part of the record and must be preserved.
